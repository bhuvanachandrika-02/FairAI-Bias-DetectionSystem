4. BIAS ANALYSIS (REAL LOGIC)

---

Add inputs:

* Male Selected
* Male Total
* Female Selected
* Female Total

Button: Analyze Bias

Logic:

Male Rate = Male Selected / Male Total
Female Rate = Female Selected / Female Total

Difference = absolute difference
Fairness Score = 100 - (difference * 100)

If difference > 0.2:
Biased, High Risk

If 0.1–0.2:
Biased, Medium Risk

If < 0.1:
Fair, Low Risk

Store globally:

* biasResult
* fairnessScore
* riskLevel
* maleRate
* femaleRate
* analysisDone = true
